Your details & consent
Your information is protected under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). Please read and accept the consents below before proceeding.
🛡️ POPIA Consent — please read carefully
Consent 1: Appointment booking Required
I consent to Nontobeko Ngcobo collecting and processing my name, email address, and mobile number for the purpose of scheduling and managing my therapy appointments.

Lawful basis: Section 11(1)(a) — Consent and Section 11(1)(b) — Performance of contract.
Retention: My contact details will be retained for 5 years from my last appointment, after which they will be anonymised.
I understand I may request access, correction, or deletion of my information at any time via My Rights.
Consent 2: Health information processing Required
The service type I select and any notes I provide constitute Special Personal Information (health data) under Section 26 of POPIA. I give explicit consent for this health-related information to be processed solely for the purpose of delivering therapy services.

This information is encrypted and will not be shared with any third party without my explicit consent, except as required by law.
Retention: 5 years, then anonymised.
Consent 3: Appointment reminders Optional
I consent to receiving appointment reminders via email and WhatsApp, sent 24 hours and 2 hours before my appointment. I understand I can withdraw this consent at any time by contacting the practice.
