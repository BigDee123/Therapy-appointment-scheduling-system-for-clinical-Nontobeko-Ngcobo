My Data Rights

Exercise your rights under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)

ℹ️ All requests are processed within 30 days as required by POPIA Section 25. You will need to provide the email address you used when booking. Requests are logged for audit purposes.
📋 Access my information Section 23
⌄

Request a full report of all personal information we hold about you, including your appointment history and consent records.

✏️ Correct my information Section 24
⌄

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you.

🗑️ Delete my information Section 24(2)(b)
⌄

Request deletion of your personal information. In accordance with healthcare record-keeping obligations, appointment records will be anonymised (personal identifiers removed) rather than hard-deleted, so that booking audit records are preserved without identifying you.

🚫 Withdraw my consent Section 11
⌄

Withdraw your consent for us to process your personal information. This will stop future appointment reminders. Note: existing confirmed appointments will still be honoured.
